WELCOME REMARKS FROM THE PRESIDENT OF THE EXECUTIVE BOARD

Dear our esteemed visitors, welcome to you all to our Website! The Center for Rule of Law Rwanda (CERULAR) while pursuing its mission to strengthen the culture of respect for the Rule of Law in Rwanda, intends to make our Website a one- stop center where you can learn of, engage with, and provide feedback on our work. We encourage you to visit our Website frequently to keep posted on our interventions. It is our pleasure to receive feedback from our visitors through the Contact Us Link. Together, we can achieve an injustice-free Rwanda!

Advocate Moses SEBUSANDI (LL.B.; LL.M)
