CERULAR - Center for Rule of law Rwanda

CERULAR is a National Non-Governmental Organisational created on 13th September 2015 and fully incorporated under Rwandan Laws regulating National Non-Governmental Organisations. The Organisation was issued its Certificate of Operation by the Rwanda Governance Board (RGB) on the 30/3/2016 through Registration Certificate No. 110/RGB/NGO/2016 and was awarded legal personality through Order No. 599/RGB/NGO/LP/06/2020.
